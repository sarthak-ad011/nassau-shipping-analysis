## 1. Shipping Lead Time Patterns
- Median lead time: 1,274 days across 10,194 shipments
- Same Day class averages 1,333 days; Standard Class averages 1,314 days
- Lead times vary across regions: Atlantic = 1,323, Gulf = 1,311, Interior = 1,323, Pacific = 1,322

## 2. Route Performance
- Most efficient routes: (1) Wicked Choccy's → Nevada (Avg LT: 1,182 days), (2) Secret Factory → Texas (Avg LT: 1,227 days), (3) Lot's O' Nuts → Virginia (Avg LT: 1,229 days)
- Worst-performing routes: (1) Wicked Choccy's → New Mexico (Avg LT: 1,489 days), (2) Lot's O' Nuts → Iowa (Avg LT: 1,479 days), (3) Secret Factory → Washington (Avg LT: 1,472 days)
- Sugar Shack (Minnesota) has the longest reach into Pacific region with only 3 Pacific orders out of 33 total

## 3. Geographic Bottlenecks
- Critical bottleneck states: Tennessee (Avg LT: 1,391, Volume: 183), Washington (Avg LT: 1,361, Volume: 506)
- High-volume + slow states (top 5): Washington, Tennessee, Indiana, Maryland, Connecticut

## 4. Ship Mode Tradeoffs
- Same Day = 1,333 days avg, but only 5.4% of orders (547 shipments)
- Standard Class = 1,314 days avg, dominant share at 60.0% (6,120 shipments)
- Same Day and First Class show slightly higher lead times than Standard, suggesting ship mode has minimal impact on actual delivery speed in this dataset

## 5. Recommendations
- Open Pacific-region distribution hub to reduce Sugar Shack → CA/OR/WA times (Sugar Shack → CA averages 1,638 days)
- Audit Standard Class for routes to New Mexico, Iowa, and Washington showing >10% above average lead time
- Investigate why Tennessee and Washington underperform despite high volume (carrier issues? geographic distance?)
- Pilot ship-mode upgrades for high-margin / high-value orders to bottleneck states
